About the Festival

Enchanted Tales Festival is a celebration of storytelling in all its forms. From captivating spoken word performances to immersive visual narratives, we bring together storytellers and audiences of all ages.

Our festival is more than just events; it's a community. A place where imagination is nurtured, creativity is sparked, and connections are forged through the power of stories.
