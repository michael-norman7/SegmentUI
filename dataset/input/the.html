About the Show

Prepare to be enchanted by 'The Great Puppet Adventure!', a brand-new production featuring a cast of whimsical characters and thrilling tales. Our show combines traditional puppetry with modern storytelling to create an immersive experience for children and adults alike.

From daring heroes to mischievous villains, each puppet is lovingly handcrafted and brought to life by our talented performers. Expect dazzling stagecraft, original music, and a whole lot of fun!

  • Suitable for all ages (recommended 3+)
  • Show duration: Approximately 60 minutes
  • Refreshments available at the venue
