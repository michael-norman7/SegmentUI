Synopsis

For centuries, the Shadowed Kingdom has been protected by an ancient magic, wielded by a lineage of powerful sorcerers. But now, the magic is fading, and a creeping darkness threatens to engulf the land. Elara, the last of her line, wants nothing to do with her heritage. Haunted by a tragic past and fearful of her own abilities, she has lived in hiding, among ordinary villagers. However, destiny has other plans. When shadows begin to stir and whispers of a forgotten evil resurface, Elara is drawn into a perilous journey. Guided by cryptic prophecies and aided by unlikely allies – a stoic knight, a mischievous rogue, and a wise old scholar – she must confront her fears and master her powers to save the kingdom from utter destruction. As Elara delves deeper into the mysteries of her ancestry, she uncovers secrets that could shatter everything she thought she knew, forcing her to make choices that will not only determine the fate of the kingdom but also her own.

About the Author
