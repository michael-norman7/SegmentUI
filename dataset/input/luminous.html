How to Use

Step 1: Cleanse

Begin with a freshly cleansed face. Gently wash your face with a mild cleanser and pat dry.

Step 2: Apply Serum

Dispense 2-3 drops of Luminous Serum onto your fingertips. Gently massage onto face and neck in upward motions.

Step 3: Moisturize & SPF

Follow with your favorite moisturizer. During the day, apply sunscreen for optimal protection.

For best results, use daily, morning and night.
