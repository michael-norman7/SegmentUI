  • Use Room Temperature Butter: Softened butter creams better with sugar, leading to a smoother dough and chewier cookies.
  • Don't Overmix: Overmixing develops gluten, which can make cookies tough. Mix until just combined.
  • Chill the Dough (Optional): For thicker cookies, chill the dough for 30 minutes before baking.
  • Underbake Slightly: Cookies continue to bake as they cool. Take them out when the edges are golden but the centers are still slightly soft.
