Vintage Travel Tips

Advice for the modern traveler with a classic soul.

Plan with Paper Maps

Embrace the charm of paper maps. They can lead to unexpected discoveries and are a beautiful keepsake of your journey.

Capture Moments on Film

Consider bringing a film camera. The deliberate process and the unique aesthetic add a nostalgic touch to your travel memories.

Journal Your Adventures

Keep a travel journal. Writing down your experiences by hand helps you connect more deeply with your journey and creates a treasured record.
