Synopsis

For centuries, the Shadowed Kingdom has been protected by an ancient magic, wielded by a lineage of powerful sorcerers. But now, the magic is fading, and a creeping darkness threatens to engulf the land. Elara, the last of her line, wants nothing to do with her heritage. Haunted by a tragic past and fearful of her own abilities, she has lived in hiding, among ordinary villagers. However, destiny has other plans. When shadows begin to stir and whispers of a forgotten evil resurface, Elara is drawn into a perilous journey. Guided by cryptic prophecies and aided by unlikely allies — a stoic knight, a mischievous rogue, and a wise old scholar — she must confront her fate and embrace her powers, to save the kingdom she fears and loves.

About the Author
